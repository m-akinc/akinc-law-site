Megan’s practice focuses on handling a wide range of estate planning and probate matters. Megan’s previous experience involves working in a boutique bankruptcy law firm as well as a prominent estate planning and estate administration firm in Fort Worth, Texas. Megan grew up in West Point, Mississippi and graduated high school from Choate Rosemary Hall in Connecticut. She obtained her undergraduate degree from the University of Texas at Austin and her J.D. magna cum laude from Texas Tech School of Law.
